HappiFoam Online Showroom

Role: Product Manager

Overview

HappiFoam’s online showroom is a modern, interactive platform where customers can browse mattresses and related sleep products. The platform supports a seamless journey from product discovery to checkout with integrated payment solutions.

Project Challenges

  • Visual Product Discovery: We emphasized immersive media (360° views, layered galleries) to enhance product appeal and reduce hesitation during purchase.
  • Frictionless Checkout: Users could check out with just three clicks via Paystack integration, increasing completed purchases by 36% within the first month.
  • Mobile First: With 82% of traffic from mobile devices, the site was fully optimized for speed, touch UX, and accessibility on mobile.

Tech Stack

WordPress CMS, Paystack API, WooCommerce
