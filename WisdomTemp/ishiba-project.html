Ishiba NGO Website

Role: Product Manager & Content Writer

Overview

Ishiba Development and Empowerment Center is one of Nigeria’s leading empowerment NGOs. The official website serves as a central hub for awareness, onboarding, and management of thousands of beneficiaries across the country.

Project Challenges

  • Streamlined Onboarding at Scale: We simplified registration forms and split them into intuitive stages, improving form completion by over 40%.
  • Accessibility & Inclusivity: With a diverse audience (many in underserved regions), we optimized the interface for both mobile and low-bandwidth environments.
  • Trust & Transparency: We published clear updates, mission metrics, and verification badges to foster public trust and donor confidence.

Tech Stack

WordPress CMS, Elementor, MySQL
